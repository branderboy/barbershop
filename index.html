Frequently Asked Questions

Can I see the space before committing?
Absolutely. We encourage a walkthrough before any commitment. Use the scheduling tool below or call Hasan directly at 202-455-3656 to book a tour.
What's the minimum lease term?
The minimum lease is 2 years. Year 1 is $2,500/month and Year 2 is $3,000/month. We're also open to discussing longer-term arrangements for the right operator.
Are utilities included in the rent?
Utility details and responsibility breakdown are covered during the walkthrough. We'll go over the full operational guidelines so there are no surprises.
Do I need my own barbering license?
Yes. The operator is responsible for maintaining all required D.C. barbering and business licenses. The space has a long history of cosmetology use, which simplifies the permitting process.
How soon can I move in?
The space is available for immediate occupancy. Once the lease is signed and the first month's payment is processed, you can move your tools in and start operating. Remember—your first month is free.
Is there parking available?
The location benefits from street parking along Florida Ave and surrounding streets. There are also nearby public parking options. Full details are discussed during the walkthrough.
